SaNdUcHe

Description

I feel that people get bored of their old sandwiches that use bread. Bread is very outdated. Why not switch to rice cakes???

Ingredients

  • 2 Puffed Crunchy Rice Cakes
  • Slice of Cheese
  • 3-4 Slices of Bologna

Steps

  1. Using a cookie cutter or knife, cut cheese into circle
  2. Assemble Sandwich: Put Bologna and Cheese in between rice cakes
  3. Go out on a walk and actually see the outside for once
