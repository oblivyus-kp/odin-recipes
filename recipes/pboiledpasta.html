Powerade-Boiled Pasta

Description

Hmmmmmmmm. Me no likely the color of pasta. ME WANT BLUE PASTA. THIS HOW TO MAKE BLUE PASTA. IT GIVE ENERGYYYYYYYYYYYYY.

Ingredients

  • Any color of powerade, blue prefferably
  • Any type of pasta

Steps

  1. Pour Powerade into a pot
  2. Turn Heat onto High and bring Powerade to a boil
  3. Add a pinch of salt into the pot
  4. Pour pasta
  5. Once pasta is cooked Al Dente, strain into strainer
  6. Voila, Greenish-Blue powerade pasta
