Soy Milk

Description

You ever get bored of regular white milk? I recommend this Soy Milk. It turns the milk brown and makes it taste better(saltier)! Its very easy to make! Try it out!

Ingredients

  • Any brand of Soy Sauce
  • A glass of milk

Steps

  1. Take the soy sauce
  2. Pour the soy sauce into the milk
  3. Mix with spoon
  4. Keep on adding soy sauce until it fits your taste
